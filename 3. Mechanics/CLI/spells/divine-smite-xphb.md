# Divine Smite
*1st-level, Evocation*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/spells/img/divine-smite.webp#right)

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

The target takes an extra `2d8` Radiant damage from the attack. The damage increases by `1d8` if the target is a Fiend or an Undead.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `1d8` for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Paladin](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 265. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
