# Cloak of Billowing
*Wondrous item, common*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/cloak-of-billowing.webp#right)


While wearing this cloak, you can take a [Bonus Action](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to make it billow dramatically for 1 minute.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 244*
