# Candle
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 1 cp

For 1 hour, a lit Candle sheds [Bright Light](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 5-foot radius and [Dim Light](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 5 feet.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 224. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
