# Cloak of the Manta Ray
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/cloak-of-the-manta-ray.webp#right)


While wearing this cloak, you can breathe underwater, and you have a [Swim Speed](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/swim-speed-xphb.md) of 60 feet.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 245. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
