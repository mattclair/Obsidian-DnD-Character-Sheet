# Gloves of Thievery
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/gloves-of-thievery.webp#right)


These gloves are imperceptible while worn. While wearing them, you gain a +5 bonus to Dexterity ([Sleight of Hand](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand)) checks.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 265. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
