# Shield of Expression
*Armor ([shield](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/shield-xphb.md)), common*  


- **Armor Class**: 2
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

The front of this Shield is shaped in the likeness of a face. While bearing the Shield, you can take a [Bonus Action](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to alter the face's expression.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 303*
