*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 2 gp
- **Weight**: 5.0 lbs.

Using a Crowbar gives you [Advantage](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Strength checks where the Crowbar's leverage can be applied.
