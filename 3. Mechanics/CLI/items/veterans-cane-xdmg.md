# Veteran's Cane
*Wondrous item, common*  



As a [Bonus Action](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can transform this walking cane into an ordinary Longsword or change the Longsword back into a walking cane. In either case, you must be holding the item.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 318*
