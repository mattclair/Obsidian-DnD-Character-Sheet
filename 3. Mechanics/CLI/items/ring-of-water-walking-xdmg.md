# Ring of Water Walking
*Ring, uncommon*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-water-walking.webp#right)


While wearing this ring, you cast [Water Walk](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/spells/water-walk-xphb.md) from it, targeting only yourself.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 296. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
