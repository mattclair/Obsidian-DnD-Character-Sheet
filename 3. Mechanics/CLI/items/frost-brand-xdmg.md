# Frost Brand
*Very rare (requires attunement)*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/frost-brand.webp#right)


When you hit with an attack roll using this magic weapon, the target takes an extra `1d6` Cold damage. In addition, while you hold the weapon, you have [Resistance](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Fire damage.

In freezing temperatures, the weapon sheds [Bright Light](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 10-foot radius and [Dim Light](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 10 feet.

When you draw this weapon, you can extinguish all nonmagical flames within 30 feet of yourself. Once used, this property can't be used again for 1 hour.
