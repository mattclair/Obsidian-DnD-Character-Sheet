# Bullseye Lantern
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 10 gp
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

A Bullseye Lantern burns Oil as fuel to cast [Bright Light](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 60-foot Cone and [Dim Light](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 60 feet.
