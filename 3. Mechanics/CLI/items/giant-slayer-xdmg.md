# Giant Slayer
*Rare*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/giant-slayer.webp#right)


You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you hit a Giant with this weapon, the Giant takes an extra `2d6` damage of the weapon's type and must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or have the [Prone](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition.
