# Ring of Telekinesis
*Ring, very rare (requires attunement)*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-telekinesis.webp#right)


While wearing this ring, you can cast [Telekinesis](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/spells/telekinesis-xphb.md) from it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 295. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
