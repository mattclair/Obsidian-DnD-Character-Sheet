# Antitoxin
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 50 gp

As a [Bonus Action](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can drink a vial of Antitoxin to gain [Advantage](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to avoid or end the [Poisoned](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 hour.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 222. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
