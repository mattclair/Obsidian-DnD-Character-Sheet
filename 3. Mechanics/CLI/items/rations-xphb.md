# Rations
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 5 sp
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

Rations consist of travel-ready food, including jerky, dried fruit, hardtack, and nuts. See "[Malnutrition](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/traps-hazards/malnutrition-xphb.md)" for the risks of not eating.
