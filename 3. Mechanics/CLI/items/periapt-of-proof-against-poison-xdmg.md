# Periapt of Proof against Poison
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  


- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This delicate silver chain has a brilliant-cut black gem pendant. While you wear it, you have [Immunity](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md) to the [Poisoned](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition and Poison damage

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 284. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
