# Efreeti Chain
*Legendary (requires attunement)*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/efreeti-chain.webp#right)


While wearing this armor, you gain a +3 bonus to [Armor Class](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md), you have [Immunity](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md) to Fire damage, and you know Primordial. In addition, you can stand on and move across molten rock as if it were solid ground.

**Variants**:
- [Efreeti Chain Mail](#Efreeti%20Chain%20Mail)
- [Efreeti Chain Shirt](#Efreeti%20Chain%20Shirt)

### Efreeti Chain Mail

- **Armor Class**: 16
- **Strength**: Requires 13 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 55.0 lbs.

### Efreeti Chain Shirt

- **Armor Class**: 13 + Dex modifier (max of +2)
- **Weight**: 20.0 lbs.


*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 257. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
