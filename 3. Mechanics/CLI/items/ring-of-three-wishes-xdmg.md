# Ring of Three Wishes
*Ring, legendary*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-three-wishes.webp#right)


While wearing this ring, you can expend 1 of its 3 charges to cast [Wish](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/spells/wish-xphb.md) from it. The ring becomes nonmagical when you use the last charge.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 296. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
