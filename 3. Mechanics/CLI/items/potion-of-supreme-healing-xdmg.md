# Potion of Supreme Healing
*Potion, very rare*  


- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You regain `10d4 + 20` [Hit Points](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) when you drink this potion. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
