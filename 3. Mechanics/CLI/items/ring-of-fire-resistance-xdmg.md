# Ring of Fire Resistance
*Ring, rare*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-resistance.webp#right)


You have [Resistance](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to fire damage while wearing this ring. The ring is set with garnet.
