# Bell
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 1 gp

When rung as a [Utilize](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action, a Bell produces a sound that can be heard up to 60 feet away.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 224. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
