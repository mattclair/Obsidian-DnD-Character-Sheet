# Crystal Ball of True Seeing
*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement)*  


- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

While touching this crystal orb, you can cast [Scrying](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/spells/scrying-xphb.md) (save DC 17) with it. In addition, you have [Truesight](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Truesight) with a range of 120 feet centered on the spell's sensor.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 159. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
