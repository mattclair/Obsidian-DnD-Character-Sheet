# Cloak of Protection
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/cloak-of-protection.webp#right)


You gain a +1 bonus to [Armor Class](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) and saving throws while you wear this cloak.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 245. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
