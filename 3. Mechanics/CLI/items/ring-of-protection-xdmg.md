# Ring of Protection
*Ring, rare (requires attunement)*  
![](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-protection.webp#right)


You gain a +1 bonus to [Armor Class](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) and saving throws while wearing this ring.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 294. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
