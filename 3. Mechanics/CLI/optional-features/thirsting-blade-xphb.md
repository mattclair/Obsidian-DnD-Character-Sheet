# Thirsting Blade
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 157. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>*  

**Prerequisite**: Level 5, pact of the blade|xphb

You gain the Extra Attack feature for your pact weapon only. With that feature, you can attack twice with the weapon instead of once when you take the [Attack](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Attack) action on your turn.
