# Magic Item Crafting Time and Cost
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 221* 

**Magic Item Crafting Time and Cost**

| Item Rarity | Time* | Cost* |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| Common | 5 days | 50 GP |
| Uncommon | 10 days | 200 GP |
| Rare | 50 days | 2,000 GP |
| Very Rare | 125 days | 20,000 GP |
| Legendary | 250 days | 100,000 GP |
^magic-item-crafting-time-and-cost

*The time and cost are halved for a consumable item other than a [Spell Scroll](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/spell-scroll-xdmg.md), whose [crafting time and cost](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/tables/scribing-spell-scrolls-spell-scroll-costs-xphb.md) are given in the "Player's Handbook".
