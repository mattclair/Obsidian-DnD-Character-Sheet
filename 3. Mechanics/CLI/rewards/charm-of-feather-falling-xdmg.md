# Charm of Feather Falling

This Charm grants you the benefits of a [Ring of Feather Falling](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/ring-of-feather-falling-xdmg.md). These benefits last for 10 days, after which the Charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 99*
