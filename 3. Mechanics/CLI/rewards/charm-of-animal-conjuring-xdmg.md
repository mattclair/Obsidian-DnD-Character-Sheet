# Charm of Animal Conjuring

This Charm allows you to cast [Conjure Animals](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-animals-xphb.md). Once used three times, the Charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 99*
