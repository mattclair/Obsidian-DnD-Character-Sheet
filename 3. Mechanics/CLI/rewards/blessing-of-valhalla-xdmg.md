# Blessing of Valhalla

This Blessing grants you the power to summon spirit warriors, as if you are blowing a [silver Horn of Valhalla](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/items/horn-of-valhalla-silver-xdmg.md). Once you use this Blessing, you can't use it again until 7 days have passed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 99*
