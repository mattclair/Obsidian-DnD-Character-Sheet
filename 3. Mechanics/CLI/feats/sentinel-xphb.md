# Sentinel
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 207*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th; Strength 13 or higher or 4th; Dexterity 13 or higher

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Guardian.** Immediately after a creature within 5 feet of you takes the [Disengage](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Disengage) action or hits a target other than you with an attack, you can make an [Opportunity Attack](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) against that creature.

**Halt.** When you hit a creature with an [Opportunity Attack](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack), the creature's Speed becomes 0 for the rest of the current turn.
