# Boon of Speed
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 211*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Escape Artist.** As a Bonus Action, you can take the [Disengage](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Disengage) action, which also ends the [Grappled](3.%20Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Grappled) condition on you.

**Quickness.** Your Speed increases by 30 feet.
